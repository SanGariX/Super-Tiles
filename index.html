FAQ

Can I play the game offline without internet?

Yes, you can play the game completely offline, especially in Human vs Human mode. This is ideal for long trips, airplane rides, or situations where you're without Wi-Fi. You can enjoy full functionality of the local multiplayer mode with just one device. Additionally, the AI mode is also available offline, so you can practice your skills anytime. Only the online multiplayer mode requires an internet connection. This makes the game a great choice for both on-the-go and stay-at-home gaming. Whether you're commuting or relaxing on the couch, the game works perfectly without needing to connect.

Is the game free or do I need to pay to play?

The game is completely free to download and play. You can access all main features including local multiplayer, AI battles, and customization options without paying anything. There are ads in the free version, but they are minimal and non-intrusive. If you prefer an ad-free experience, you can make a one-time in-app purchase to remove them permanently.
