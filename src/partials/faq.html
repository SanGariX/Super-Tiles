FAQ

Can I play the game offline without internet?

Yes, you can play the game completely offline, especially in Human vs Human mode. This is ideal for long trips, airplane rides, or situations where you're without Wi-Fi. You can enjoy full functionality of the local multiplayer mode with just one device. Additionally, the AI mode is also available offline, so you can practice your skills anytime. Only the online multiplayer mode requires an internet connection. This makes the game a great choice for both on-the-go and stay-at-home gaming. Whether you're commuting or relaxing on the couch, the game works perfectly without needing to connect.

Is the game free or do I need to pay to play?

The game is completely free to download and play. You can access all main features including local multiplayer, AI battles, and customization options without paying anything. There are ads in the free version, but they are minimal and non-intrusive. If you prefer an ad-free experience, you can make a one-time in-app purchase to remove them permanently. There are no hidden subscriptions or recurring fees. It’s a fair model that lets players choose how they want to experience the game. Everyone can enjoy it without limitations.

How do I remove ads from the game?

To remove ads, you can make a simple one-time in-app purchase directly from the main menu. Once purchased, ads will no longer appear in any part of the game. This means uninterrupted play sessions, especially useful during multiplayer or intense matches. The purchase is tied to your Google account, so you can restore it on any other device using the same account. There are no additional purchases required once you buy the ad-free version. It’s a one-and-done upgrade. The developers wanted to keep things simple and fair for all players.

What Android version is required to run the game?

The game supports devices running Android 7.0 and above. It’s designed to work smoothly on both older and newer Android phones and tablets. Performance is optimized so that even mid-range devices can handle the game without lag. The app is lightweight and doesn’t require a lot of storage or memory to run. This makes it accessible to a wide range of users. You don’t need the latest flagship phone to enjoy the game. As long as your device meets the minimum requirement, you’re good to go.

Can I customize the game board or appearance?

Yes! The game includes several board themes and background color options. You can select your favorite style from the settings menu before starting a match. These themes add variety and make the experience more personal. Whether you like vibrant colors or more muted tones, there’s a theme for you. For now, you can continue where you left off as long as you keep the game installed. Most casual players find this system more than sufficient. It keeps things simple and straightforward.
